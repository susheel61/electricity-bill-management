# Electricity Bill Management System

A comprehensive web application designed to simplify the management and tracking of electricity bills for multiple tenants and owners. Built with modern web technologies, it offers a seamless user experience with a premium design.

- **Dashboard Overview**: Get a quick snapshot of total dues, paid bills, and unpaid bills.
- **Bill Tracking**: View detailed bill information including amount, due date, and payment status.
- **History View**: Access historical billing data organized by month and year.
- **Search & Filter**: Easily find bills by name, USN, or service number, and filter by payment status.
- **Responsive Design**: Fully optimized for mobile, tablet, and desktop devices.
- **Dark Mode**: A sleek, premium dark theme for comfortable viewing.
- **Tenant Management**: (Coming Soon) Manage tenant details and room assignments.
